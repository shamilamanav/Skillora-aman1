Welcome to Skillora

It’s time to find intelligence and build up a career.

This project was developed as part of the Hackathon program in the Art Fest Kauthukam 2k25, proudly presented by Omega House. The platform introduces an innovative chatbot designed to help students explore their unique intelligence types, based on Howard Gardner’s Theory of Multiple Intelligences.

Specifically tailored for the students of Darul Huda, the chatbot not only identifies individual intelligence profiles but also provides personalized study methods and career guidance aligned with each learner’s strengths. It acts as a companion for students who want to understand themselves better and discover career paths that match their potential.

Beyond academics, Skillora encourages holistic growth by fostering creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, and confidence among learners. The vision behind this project is to make education more inclusive, student-centered, and future-ready, ensuring that every learner finds value and direction in their educational journey.
